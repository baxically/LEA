Castro Valley Farmers' Market

Farmers' market that takes place every Saturday from 10AM-2PM. Some vendors are Achadinha Cheese, Avila Farms, Sunrise Farms, Froylan Flower Farm, Hummus Guy, and Poke Bowl!

Corner of Redwood Rd. and Norbridge Ave

Fremont Festival of the Arts

Arts & craft festival in Fremont- also has a ton of food, drinks performers, rides and more!

Downtown Fremont

San Rafael Civic Center Farmers' Market

Farmers' market that takes place Sundays and Thursdays from 8AM-1PM. The market includes local farmers, artisans, and more local vendors!

3501 Civic Center Dr San Rafael CA

White Elephant Sale

Northern California's biggest annual rummage sale. There is a bus that takes people from the closest BART station to the event

White Elephant Sale Warehouse
333 Lancaster Street, Oakland, CA
