MedShare

This humanitarian aid organization collects and distributes surplus medical supplies to over 100 countries. Bay Area volunteers can support MedShare at its Western Region Distribution Center.

2937 Alvarado St, San Leandro, CA 94577

One Warm Coat

Just as its name describes, this organization hosts coat drive events nationwide. Individuals can donate their coats at drives near their location or even start their own.

Mailing Address:
2443 Fillmore St. #380-5363
San Francisco, CA 94115

Open Heart Kitchen

Serving the Tri-Valley area, Open Heart Kitchen provides healthy meals for anyone who needs them. Anyone over ten years old is welcome to volunteer for this amazing non-profit.

5200 Case Ave, Pleasanton, CA 94566
They also serve at various locations in Dublin, Pleasanton, and Livermore.

Rise Against Hunger

This international organization provides food and aid to those who need it most. Volunteers in the Bay Area can package meals and perform other tasks to do their part to end world hunger.

2296 Tripaldi Way
Hayward, CA 94545
