Mission Statement
LEA stands for local, ethical and affordable. We're here to give students and community members in the Bay Area a resource to find stores and restaurants that fit around our motto.

Why LEA?
We, the founders, are students in the Bay Area and were inspired to create an outlet that we couldn't find.
